Responsive Parallax Narrative

Slide 1

There once was a young man named Andy. He was tall and fit and loved adventures. One day he decided he wanted to explore somewhere new and unheard of before, the Magical Enchanted Forrest. He set off for the mountains.

Upon arrival, the mountains seemed like normal mountains from the outside. It was much quieter and the trees were thick but all the same. It was as though the mountains did not want anyone to know their secrets that there was something more here. ...But Andy knew. He had heard of this legendary forrest from his grandfather who had been told by his dad who was told by his dad and it keeps going on for many a generations.

Andy started walking forward and with one deep breath stepped one foot forward into the dark forrest. He could not believe what he was seeing. There was nothing around. Andy thought for a second he must've gotten the location wrong and gone to the incorrect mountain. That thought was short lasting. Looking around the forrest Andy spottet out of the corner of his eye what appeared to be a multicolor fairy, flying quickly from tree to sky.

He was finally here, he could not believe his eyes. Soon out of the shadows appeared more magical animals. There was a unicorn prancing around him, a pegasis was flying through the sky and water was being moved through the air, possibly because of a wizard.

Andy continued his adventure through the forrest. With each step forward he could feel more and more of the magic that surrounded him. The magical space was engulfing him. Would he ever be the same again, Andy thought not. After what fell like forever Andy found himself at the top of a massive lake that waterfalled off a cliff. Andy thought this would make for a great resting place to enjoy the beauty and take a break to eat.
